 Welcome to 

Holly Promise School For The Disabled

Holly Promise School for the disabled is an inclusive and a well rounded education programme that reflects our deeply held belief that all pupils have special gifts, talents and needs. Our commitment as educators is to bring out these gifts in each and every one of our pupils; to help each pupil find their own voice.

It has the aim to provide high quality education inorder to improve living standards of persons living with disabilities in Zimbabwe and also the life of the less privileged.

We would want to provide free education,accommodation and free food to children with learning barriers from up to the age of 20 years,covering epilepsy,albinism, down sysndromes,deaf and dump,blind,orphans and street kids

 Objectives for Holly Promise 

* Running a learning institution from Early Childhood development to form 6 in an inclusive environment

* Establishing a rehabilitation centre for severly handicapped children and introducing all learners despite impairments to core curriculum activities

* Fundraising through the business world,NGOs,loans,grants etc to buy reading books, furniture, and find easier means of transport to ferry staff members and children on wheelchairs,crutches to and from school  

* To source funds to puchase and construct school land that will accommodate Primary and Secondary students

* To raise funds for food for 15 children living with diasbilities currently at the school

* To raise funds to buy wheelchairs, learning aids,brailers,crutches, sun creams and sun hats for albinos
